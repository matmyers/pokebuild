What kind of team would you like to build?









Not sure? If you are building a competitive team, you're most likely looking for Standard Competitive. If you're building for a draft league, you'll know.

The In-game option allows you to plan a team for a playthrough of a game (such as Gold version or Moon version) without worrying about items and EVs. This will provide you with the in-game locations of all pokémon obtainable in the game you're planning for, along with their movesets.

Still want the EVs and other competitive info for your in-game team? You can plan an in-game team with the In-game teambuilder, and then export it to the Standard Competitive teambuilder.
